Who we are

Our team of professionals has deep knowledge and experience with a wide variety of commercial property types, including office, industrial, retail, hospitality, multifamily and age-restricted residences, and in other real estate-related assets, and on all tranches of the capital stack, as well as with respect to land-use, entitlements, development, management and construction. Why Us

We accomplish our investment objectives by executing on a dual strategy of acquiring existing stabilized cash flowing assets at below their replacement costs to support stable, consistent, distributions and simultaneously acquiring opportunistic assets which we can reposition, redevelop or remarket to create value enhancement and capital appreciation.

Why Real Estate

There are a number of options for investors looking to diversify their portfolios while creating long-term income and asset growth. Stocks, bonds and cash equivalents are the most common investment choices. In addition to stocks, bonds and mutual funds, real estate should be viewed as a significant asset class. Investing in real estate gives individuals the opportunity to grow their income along through building and land development.

About

First Capital through its affiliates, acts as sponsor to First Capital Real Estate Trust Incorporated, a public Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT). Supporting the successful formation and launch of First Capital Real Estate Trust, First Capital, through its affiliates, has been responsible for obtaining SEC, FINRA and Blue-Sky approvals, partnering with an affiliated FINRA-registered Broker-Dealer, and building a dedicated national sales team to sell shares to the public.

First Capital's team of professionals has deep knowledge and experience with a wide variety of commercial property types, including office, industrial, retail, hospitality, multifamily and age-restricted residences, and in other real estate related assets, and on all tranches of the capital stack, as well as with respect to land-use, entitlements, development, management and construction. Whether it's investing for our own account, with partners, or assisting clients in achieving their goals, First Capital mobilizes the core competence of its entire team of professionals to conceive of the best strategies to achieve the goals for each project, and tirelessly execute on that strategic plan to achieve superior risk adjusted results. In response to its foreign capital raising initiative, First Capital is expanding coverage to other global markets including Japan and Asia, Europe, the Middle East and South America.
